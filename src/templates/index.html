Frequently asked questions

The application aims to automate bus route management, student registration, and seat allocation for multiple educational institutions. It provides centralized control for admins, real-time seat availability, and seamless communication for students, parents, and school administrators.

The system is intended for central admins, institutional admins and students/parents. Central admins manage all buses and routes, while institutional admins manage registrations and bookings for their specific institutions.

  • Centralized bus management by central admins.
  • Automated seat allocation.
  • Real-time seat availability checking and advance booking.
  • Dynamic route adjustments.
  • Special scheduling options.

Students or parents select routes, pickup/drop-off points, and timing options based on available buses. Successful bookings trigger confirmation emails sent to the registered email address.
